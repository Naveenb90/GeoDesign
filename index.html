Why Soil Testing Matters?

📍

Site Access

Quality of access impacts site investigation.

🧱

Type of Soil

Clay is reactive, sand settles under load, silt loses strength when wet.

🌳

Trees & Roots

Roots may draw moisture, causing shrinkage near footings.

🪨

Rock Boulders

May move underground or create voids when excavated.

⚠️

Soft Soil

Can settle under load affecting stability.

🌊

Marine Clays

May have low or no bearing capacity.

⛰️

Slopes

Steeper slopes may have slip potential.

💧

Low-Lying Areas

Could contain acid sulfate soils.

🛠️

Filled Areas

May have un-compacted soils causing settlement.

⚡

Cut & Fill Pads

Could experience differential settlement.

💦

Water Table

Depths can affect excavation feasibility.

🪨

Rock Layers

May impact excavation.

🚰

Wastewater Suitability

Soil suitability for sewerage and greywater disposal.

🏞️

Land Block

Suitability for on-site wastewater disposal.

Our Services

Soil Testing for All Types of Constructions

Comprehensive soil investigation to determine bearing capacity, settlement behavior, and suitability for buildings, roads, and industrial projects.

Laboratory Soil Testing

State-of-the-art soil mechanics laboratory for analyzing strength, composition, and moisture properties that influence design and performance.

Pile Foundation & Foundation Design

Expert design and execution of deep foundations to support high-rise buildings, bridges, and heavy structures.

Plate Load Test

On-site testing to evaluate soil bearing capacity and settlement characteristics for shallow foundations.

Pile Load Test / Pull Out Test

Critical testing to validate the load-carrying capacity and stability of piles, ensuring reliability in foundation systems.

Electrical Resistivity Test

Subsurface investigation to map soil stratification, groundwater, and rock layers using resistivity methods for better foundation planning.

Topographical & Contour Survey

Precise land surveys to capture terrain features and elevation data, supporting site planning, drainage design, and infrastructure projects.
